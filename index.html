About Us

The Dragon.Stilts Story

We started Dragon.Stilts in 2024 with a simple idea: performers deserve equipment that keeps up with their art. After years of building stilts in our workshop for local circus troupes, demand grew — and so did we.

Today we supply performers, theater companies, event agencies, and street artists across Europe and beyond. Every pair of stilts leaves our workshop tested, adjusted, and ready to perform.
